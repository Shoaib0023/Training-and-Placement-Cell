Director's Message

Dear Students,
Rajkiya Enginnering College is marking its presence as an emerging Technical institude which is committed to inculcate the ideas of enterpreneurship, academics, integretity and accountability among the students and to show them the right path for their implementation.

REC-K, a place where we celebrate youth and excellence and Endeavour to modify young person into adult with sense of social obligations, human values and bear on environment. We not only strive to train the students to become Scientist, leaders, thinkers and technologist of the society but also help to become human beings. n the quest of knowledge and with a mission to empower the students in their journey to become excellent human resources and to contribute meaningfully to the City, Nation and the human society, REC-K has started its services. It offers wide range of educational programs in the field of Technology.It has a rich tradition of pursuing academic excellence, value based education and providing a conducive environment for overall personality development of students. In addition to their excellent teaching, the faculty members are actively involved in the grooming of the students.

We provide high standard of teaching-learning process offered by qualified teachers using state-of-the-art laboratories. Our objective is to create a breed of qualified, innovative and dynamic professionals for corporate sectors, the service industry, for self-employment and for academic and research institutions of socio-economic importance. We strongly believe in empowering women. Accordingly, our teaching-learning process is based on motivating factors, discipline, although in relaxed natural ambience. We are determined to help our students to understand the power of words, appreciate the beauty of languages, and learn the art, science and technology. , in fracture, curriculum, faculty and students i.e. four basic component of education system are perfectly blended to ensure our vision. We have created a secured, congenial and serene environment for both boys and girls students to make it their coveted educational destination. And we are only in the process of further development.

Wishing the student good luck and best time at REC-K.

Prof. Neelam Srivastava

Director, REC Kannauj
