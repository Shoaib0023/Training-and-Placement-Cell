About Us

Rajkiya Enginnering College, Kannauj (RECK) is an institude with total commitment to quality and exellence in acedamics. It was established by Government of Uttar Pradesh in the year 2015 with a single branch Electrical Enginnering with annual intake of Sixty(60) students running at Harcout Butler Technical University, Kanpur (formerely known as HBTI, Kanpur). In 2016 three more branches have been added namely Electronics Enginnering, Civil Enginnering, Computer Science & Enginnering with annual intake of Sixty(60) in each branch.
